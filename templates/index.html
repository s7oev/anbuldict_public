This is my final CS50 project, and more!

One day, if/when I become an entrepreneur, I have decided to name my company using an Ancient Bulgarian word that would relate to its activity (many different ideas as to what that activity will be). So, often when I get an idea, I would summarize it in one word and try to find its translation in Ancient Bulgarian. However, this is not too easy: there is a dictionary from Ancient Bulgarian to (Modern) Bulgarian, thanks to Sofia University, but not the reverse (Bulgarian to Ancient Bulgarian). So, here is the first one... with the added benefit of English to Ancient Bulgarian translation!
